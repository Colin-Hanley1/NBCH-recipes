---
name: lemon-herb roasted salmon and quinoa bowls
meal: lunch
time: 8
tags: [pescatarian,no-cook]
---

## ingredients
- 1 lb salmon fillet
- 1 cup dry quinoa
- 2 cups vegetable broth
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp dried oregano
- 1 lemon, thinly sliced

## steps
prepare grains: rince quinoa under cold water. combine with vegetable broth in a pot, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer, covered, for 15 minutes. fluff with a fork and let cool
roast: preheat oven to 400 F. place salmon fillet and broccoli florets on a baking sheet. drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. place lemon slices directly on top of the salmon
cook: bake for 12-15 minutes until the salmon flakes easily with a fork and broccoli is tender-crisp
