name: smoked salmon and cream cheese pinwheels
meal: lunch

tags: [pescatarian,no-cook]
---

## ingredients
- 1 large whole wheat tortilla
- 2 tsp plain cream cheese
- 2 slices smoked salmon
- 1 tbsp capers, drained
- 1 handfull fresh baby spinach
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

## steps
spread: lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface. spread the cream cheese in an even, thin layer across the entire surface of the tortilla, leaving about a half-inch border at the edges
layer: place the spinach leaves over the cream cheese. lay the smoked salmon slices over the spinach, ensuring they are distributed evenly
finish: sprinkle the cpaers and black pepper over the salmon
roll: starting from one side, roll the tortilla tightly into a cylinder
portion: use a serrated knife to cut the log into 1-inch thick rounds
