---
name: spicy tuna rice bowl
meal: lunch
time: 20
tags: [pescatarian]
---

## ingredients
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 2 cans tuna in water, drained
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp sriracha
- 1 cup edamame, shelled and thawed
- 1 large carrot, julienned or shredded
- 1 sheet nori
- 1 tsp sesame oil

## steps
mix dressing: in a bowl, combine the drained tuna, mayonnaise, sriracha, and sesame oil. mix until it forms a creamy, spicy tuna paste
prep base: divide the chilled cooked rice
assemble: tip one side of the rice with the spicy tuna mixture, arrange the edamame and shredded carrots in separate sections, then top with nori
