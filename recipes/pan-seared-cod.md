---
name: pan-seared cod with lemon-garlic butter
meal: dinner
time: 20
tags: [pescatarian]
---

## ingredients
- 2 cod fillets (or any white fish)
- 2 tbsp butter (or olive oil)
- 3 minced garlic cloves
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1/2 tsp paprike

## steps
prep fish: pat the cod fillets completely dry with paper towels. season both sides generously with salt, pepper, and paprika
sear: melt 1 tbsp of butter into a large skillet over medium-high heat. once bubbling, add the fillets. cook undisturbed for 3-4 minutes per side until the edges are golden brown and the fish flakes easily. Remove fish from the pan
make sauce: reduce heat to medium/ add the remaining tablespoon of butter and the minced garlic to the same pan. Saute for 1 minute until fragrant
finish: stir in the lemon juice and parsley. pour this sauce over the cooked fish and serve immediately
