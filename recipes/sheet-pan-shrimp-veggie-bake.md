name: sheet-pan shrimp and mediterranean veggie bake
meal: lunch
time: 25
tags: [pescatarian]

## ingredients
- 1 lb medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 zucchini, sliced into half moons
- 1 bell pepper, chopped into 1-inch pieces
- 1 red onion, chopped into 1-inch pieces
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp cumin

## steps
season: preheat oven to 425 F. In a large bowl, toss the shrimp,zucchini, bell pepper, and onion with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, cumin, salt, and pepper
roast: spread the mixture in a single layer on a large rimmed baking sheet. Roast for 10-12 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and opaque and the veggies are tender
