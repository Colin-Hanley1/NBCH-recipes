## ingredients
- 1 can of drained tuna
- 1/2 cup canned cannellini beans, drained
- 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
- 1 tbsp EVOO
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1/4 tsp dried oregano
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1 cup baby spinach or arugula

## steps
combine base: in a medium mixing bowl, flake the drained tuna with a fork. add the rinsed cannellini beans and diced red onion
season: add the olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, and black pepper. stir thoroughly until the ingredients are evenly coated
assemble: if packing into a mason jar, layer the spinach at the bottom, followed by the tuna and bean mixture on top.
