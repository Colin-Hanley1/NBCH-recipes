---
name: one-pot creamy spinach and shrimp pasta
meal: dinner
time: 25
tags: [pescatarian]
---

## ingredients
- 8 oz linguine or penne
- 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 cups fresh baby spinach
- 2 minced garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 1/3 cup grated parmesean 
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- 2 cups vegetable broth
  
## steps
boil: in a large pot, bring the vegetable broth to a boil. add the pasta and cook until al dente
add protein: when there are three minutes of cooking time left for the pasta, add the shrimp directly into the pot with the pasta and broth
simmer: once the shrimp are pink, stir in the garlic, red pepper flakes, and heavy cream. let simmer for 1 minute to thicken slightly
wilt and serve: remove from heat and stir in the fresh spinach and parmesan.
