meal: dinner

tags: [pescatarian, vegetarian]

## ingredients
- 1 head cauliflower, cut into small florets
- 1 can chickpeas, rinsed, drained, patted dry
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 6-8 small corn tortillas
- toppings of choice

## steps
season: preheat oven to 425. on a large baking sheet, toss the cauliflower and chickpeas with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper
roast: bake for 20-25 minutes, tossing halfway through, until the cauliflower is tender and the chickpeas are slight
warm: in the last 3 minutes of roasting, wrap the corn tortillas in foil and place them in the oven to warm through
