name: mediterranean sardine and cucumber pita
meal: lunch
time: 8
tags: [pescatarian,no-cook]
---

## ingredients
- 1 can sardines in olive oil
- 1 whole wheat pita
- 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced into thin half-moons
- 2 tbsp crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tsp fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 squeeze of lemon wedge

## steps
prep fish: open the sardine can and break them into bite-size chunks
season: add the lemon juice and fresh parsley to the sardines and toss gently
assemble: open the pita pickets and layer the cucumber slices first to provide a crunch barrier
fill: spoon the sardine mixture into the pita pockets over the cucumbers
finish: sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese into each pocket
